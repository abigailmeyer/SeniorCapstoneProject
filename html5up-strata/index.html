Hello! I'm Abigail and this is my Senior Capstone Project, "Where are you from?".

This is a multimedia interview based project that discusses the challanges and experiences that third culture kids face throughout their lifetime.

Where are you from?

Throughout my time at Fordham as a New Media and Information student I have gained a lot of different skills that will benefit me for the rest of my professional career. My major has allowed me to take a variety of classes including basic coding courses, visual arts courses, and much more.

Developing an idea for this project was really challenging because I hit a bit of a creative block at the beginning of the semester, but eventually came up with something.

I grew up with two parents who worked for the government and I have moved 12 times so I’ve never really had a sense of “home”. My parents always called my siblings and I “Third Culture Kids” but I never really understood the termination until I recently read a book titled ‘I’m From...Earth?’.

Third culture kids or third culture individuals are people who were raised in a culture other than their parents' or the culture of their country of nationality, and also live in a different environment during a significant part of their child development years.

I decided to develop an interview based, multimedia project that would consist of interviews with other Third Culture Kids, to create a space where third culture kids can talk about their experiences and more. The website is coded by me and there is no use of a third party platform.
